Manthan Achievements

Manthanites participated and won 3rd prize in the First State wide Theatre Competitions conducted by Sangeet Natak Academi held at Ravindra Bharathi Auditorium, in Hyderabad. Manthan performed Tagore's Tota kahini (Parrot's Tale) a satire on how the education system of the colonized paid no heed to the cultural traits inherent to Indians. 400 high schools from all over Telangana participated in these competitions. Manthan has made it to be one among the 26 schools that entered the final round of the competition.

They seem to comprehend their speeches very well too. Appreciate your English Language teachers for the training imparted to the students and hearty Congratulations to the students. - Renu Chamarty

Campus life

Library
Has 21,000 book titles

Manthan takes pride in encouraging and inspiring children to read from a very young age. It is our strong belief that reading enhances one's life both, academically and socially. As a school, we strive to make each student develop the zeal to read and enjoy different literary works. Our philosophy towards reading can be summarized in a wonderful quote by Frank Serafini - “There is no such thing as a child who hates to read; there are only children who have not found the right book.”



Science Labs
Hands on experience

Our science labs allow students to gain hands-on experience of what they learn in the classroom. Throughout the course of every unit, we ensure students get an opportunity for practical work in the lab in all three sciences, Physics, Chemistry and Biology.



Computer labs
Techonologically Advanced

Our labs are designed so that every student can access and work on individual computers during ICT classes. Our computers are state of the art and include all the necessary software for students to utilize during their classes. The labs also include a projector, smart board and a computer system. Furthermore, our computers are safe and allow internet access only for educational purposes. In addition, the labs are only used under the strict supervision of a teacher.



Whats New

  • Debate And Music Club

    Dance and Western Music are some of the many high school clubs available at Manthan. Children are exposed to varied kinds of music, and dance forms, and encouraged to formulate pieces of music and choreograph steps on their own. Children in the younger grades have Dance and Western Music classes incorporated into their daily schedules, allowing them to unwind and express themselves in the most creative way possible! During assemblies, festival celebrations, or the grand annual day event, children get to showcase their skills through melodious musical performances, and dances to peppy tracks.

  • Student Leadership

    At Manthan, we take pride in the fact that students of Grades 6 to 12 elect their house leaders in a democratic fashion. Captains and Vice Captains for each of the 4 houses - Agni, Amber, Prithvi and Vayu - are elected at the beginning of the academic year. These 8 captains, in turn, elect the 2 school captains though a single transferable voting system. These elections are fiercely contested with posters, speeches, nominations, withdrawals, ballots etc. akin to political elections. Students in the highest grade play the role of the election commission and ensure that the process is fair and smooth.
